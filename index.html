Project Timeline

Streamline you vision with this media project timeline

  • Phase 1

    Plan

    The slate is clean and you have ideas!
    Organize by importance and begin to formulate a structure for your presentation. Draw or write out the idea and create a timeline (with plenty of time!) that has due dates and checkpoints.
    With a solid idea for the product you want to create, it's time to capture your media

  • Phase 2

    Create your Media

    Get the devices, data, software, and space needed to capture your project's media!
    Not sure what your media might need? Tell us about your project and we'll point you in the right direction.

  • Phase 3

    Production

    Time to edit, clean, proofread, and organize your media or data! This essential step will take time and effort: stay organized and focus on the central message of your project.
    Check out our tutorials for editing and producing tips!

  • Phase 4

    Presenting

    Your t's are cross and your i's are dotted. Leave time to practice and review your project with a friend before your final presentation.
